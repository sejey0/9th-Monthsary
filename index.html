Happy 9th Monthsary

My love, my favorite person 🌸

♡

My dearest love, it's been nine beautiful months since you walked into my life and turned everything into something I never want to lose. Nine months of laughter, of late nights, of the small stolen moments that have become my favorite memories.

I still remember the way my heart skipped the very first time — and somehow, impossibly, it still does every single time I see you. You make ordinary days feel like a dream I'm terrified to wake up from.

Thank you for every "good morning," every hug that lasted just a little longer than it needed to, every time you held my hand and made me feel like the luckiest person in the world. Thank you for being patient, warm, and endlessly kind — even on the days I made it hard.

Nine months feels like a whole lifetime with you, and yet somehow, I want a thousand more. I want every season, every chapter, every ordinary Tuesday — as long as it's with you.

Here's to us. To the nine months behind us, and to all the beautiful ones still to come. 💜
